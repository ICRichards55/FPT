How to Screen Record
Tap "Go Fullscreen" first, then:
1
Tap Go Fullscreen — the overlay fills your iPhone screen
2
Swipe down from the top-right corner to open Control Center
3
Tap the Screen Record button (circle inside a circle) — a 3-second countdown begins
4
Let the video play through, then tap the red clock in the top-left to stop recording
5
Open Photos → find the recording → tap Edit to trim the ends if needed → post directly to Instagram
💡 Don't see Screen Record in Control Center? Go to iPhone Settings → Control Center → Add Screen Recording
